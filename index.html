Memoir

Prof. (Dr.) Purvi Pokhariyal is a distinguished academician and legal professional, currently serving as the Dean of the School of Law, Forensic Justice & Policy Studies, and as the Director of the Log Nayak Jai Prakash Narayan Institute of Criminology and Forensic Science (LNJN-NICFS), National Forensic Sciences University (NFSU), Delhi campus. She also holds the Director, Academic, Research & Consultancy position at NFSU. She is recognized for her expertise in Criminal Justice Studies and Forensic Justice Education.
